About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am Xiaxue Ouyang, a Ph.D. candidate (2023-) at the Institute of Statistics and Big Data, Renmin University of China. I have been pursuing my doctoral studies under the supervision of Dr. Cheng Meng at BDAL. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from Wuhan University in 2023.

My research interests lie broadly in statistical machine learning, with a particular focus on optimal transport, feature screening, and subsampling methods.
